HOTELS & TRAVEL

Hotels

Our wedding is in a city centre location, and there are many hotels within walking distance to suit any budget. You may wish to book a room at the Midland Hotel (with a limited number of rooms available at a special rate, please mention the White-Stuart wedding). Alternatively, there is a Premier Inn opposite the Midland with many other hotels less than 5 minute walk away.

Car

If travelling by car, please note that the sat nav postcode for Manchester Town Hall is M2 5DB and The Midland Hotel is M2 3NQ. Directions are available for the Midland Hotel. There is currently a diversion in place due to nearby tram improvement works. There is limited on street parking available. Alternatively there are several nearby NCP carparks.

Train

If travelling by train into Manchester Piccadilly or Victoria, you can get a Metrolink tram to Deansgate or St Peter's Square. St Peter's Square is currently undergoing improvement works, but is provisionally scheduled to reopen in August 2016. Please see the Metrolink website for updates. There is also a free Metroshuttle service, linking the city centre with the mainline train stations.
